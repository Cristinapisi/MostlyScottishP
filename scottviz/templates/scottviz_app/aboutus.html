{% extends "scottviz_app/base.html" %} {% block content %} We are 5 MSc Computing Science Students at the University of Glasgow and this is our Team Project!

This website is supposed to help people have easier access to statistics and visualise data extracted from the votes of the Members of the Scottish Parliament.

The data is extracted from the official Scottish Parliament website. As we realise that we cannot provide all the visualisations users might want, we hope to allow for algorithms to be uploaded and used to render the data visualy.

Our goals:
  • We aim to update the data weekly
  • We will provide some visualisations
  • We will provide a framework for users to upload their own algorithms
