  • Fort William to host next Parliament Day

    Fort William will be the home of the Scottish Parliament when it plays host to Parliament Day on 24 November.

    The Local Government & Regeneration Committee and the Devolution (Further Powers) Committee will undertake a series of meetings with local organisations & groups as well as speaking to 16 and 17 year olds who recently voted in the referendum.
